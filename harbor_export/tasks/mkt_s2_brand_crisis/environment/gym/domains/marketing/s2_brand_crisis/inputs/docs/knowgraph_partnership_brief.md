- **Partner:** KnowGraph Education (Delaware C-Corp; primary office San
  Mateo CA; secondary office London UK).

- **Product integration:** KnowGraph's curriculum content API ("KG-Core")

  Lumalynx products and in joint marketing. Lumalynx does **not**
  represent or warrant KnowGraph's upstream content provenance — that
  warranty flows from KnowGraph to Lumalynx under Section 7.3 of the
  Content License Addendum.

## The Verge allegations (published 2026-03-27 at 18:02 ET)

The Verge alleges that KnowGraph's problem-set bank contains material
scraped from a list of copyrighted K-8 textbook publishers without
licensing. The story names three publisher plaintiffs who are reportedly
preparing a joint letter to KnowGraph. Lumalynx is mentioned once — in
the seventh paragraph — as "the largest K-12 customer of KnowGraph's
content API."

The story does not allege wrongdoing by Lumalynx. It does allege that
Lumalynx's product includes KnowGraph content.

## Our exposure (from Devika's initial read)

1. **FTC Section 5 / substantiation.** Any public statement Lumalynx
   makes defending KnowGraph's training data provenance is effectively a
   claim Lumalynx does not have substantiation to make. Devika's hard
   line: we do not defend facts we do not hold.
2. **FTC endorsement guides.** The 18-month co-marketing history means
   any Lumalynx statement about KnowGraph is effectively a testimonial,
   which carries its own substantiation requirement.
3. **COPPA / children's-content framing.** K-8 audience. Every word will
   be read through a children's-content ethics lens.
4. **UK ASA + EU advertising rules.** KnowGraph's London office pulls
   the Guardian into scope. Different rules from FTC; Fiona owns the
   EMEA track with a separate CLM envelope (`claims_review_uk`).
5. **Contractual remedy.** Section 7.3 gives Lumalynx the right to
   demand a written factual response from KnowGraph within 10 business
   days and to reserve termination rights if the response is materially
   incomplete. This is the spine of the Day-3 partner letter.

## What the partner letter must do

- Acknowledge the story without conceding legal liability on either
  side.
- Request KnowGraph's written factual response within 10 business days
  per Section 7.3.
- Preserve the contractual relationship while reserving Lumalynx's
  rights under Sections 7.3 and 12.1.
- Be routed through a distinct DocuSign CLM envelope
  (`partner_letter_review`) and reviewed by Devika before send.
- Be signed by Margo.

- Concede liability on Lumalynx's part.
- Endorse KnowGraph's training data practices or provenance.
- Quote the CEO (Raj Venkataraman) — he is not the signatory of record
