## 1. Scope of Services

StagePro Events shall provide the following services for LumaSummit 2026:

### 1.1 Main Stage Production
- Main stage (40 ft x 24 ft) with custom LED backdrop (16 ft x 9 ft, 2.6mm pixel pitch)
- Front-of-house audio: line array system (d&b audiotechnik or equivalent), 8 wireless handheld microphones, 4 wireless lavalier microphones, 2 podium microphones
- Front-of-house lighting: 48 LED wash fixtures, 24 LED profile spots, 4 followspots, programmable lighting console
- 2 confidence monitors (32-inch) for speakers at stage lip
- 1 presentation laptop (Windows) and 1 presentation laptop (Mac) with seamless switching
- Live camera feed: 3 cameras (1 wide, 1 tight, 1 roaming), switched to IMAG screens (2 x 16 ft x 9 ft flanking stage)
- Teleprompter for keynote sessions (2 glass panels)

### 1.2 Breakout Room AV (6 rooms)
- Each room: projector (6,000+ lumens), screen (10 ft x 6 ft), wireless presentation system, 2 wireless microphones, basic lighting
- Room capacity: 80-200 per room depending on configuration
- Speaker timer/countdown display in each room

### 1.3 Interactive Demo Stage (Expo Hall)
- Demo stage (20 ft x 12 ft) with dedicated AV system
- Multi-screen setup: 3 x 65-inch interactive displays with touch capability
- Dedicated presentation system supporting live software demos with real-time audience interaction
- Audience polling hardware (200 polling devices) integrated with main screen
- High-bandwidth dedicated internet connection (minimum 100 Mbps symmetric) for live product demos

### 1.4 Virtual Overflow Streaming
- Live streaming of all main stage sessions to Hopin virtual platform
- 3 camera feeds plus presentation capture
- Dedicated streaming engineer for the duration of the event
- Pre-event streaming test (T-7 days)
- Estimated virtual overflow attendees: 200-300

### 1.5 Speaker Technical Support
- Dedicated speaker-ready room with AV check setup (replica of main stage presentation system)
- 1 technical director on-site for duration of event
- 2 AV technicians per breakout room per day
- 1 dedicated demo stage technician
- Speaker laptop compatibility testing (T-14 days, remote; T-1 day, on-site)

### 1.6 Load-In / Load-Out Schedule
- Load-in: July 11 (08:00 CT) through July 13 (22:00 CT) — 3 full days
- Technical rehearsal: July 13 (14:00-22:00 CT) — main stage and demo stage
- Event days: July 14-16
- Load-out: July 17 (06:00 CT) through July 17 (22:00 CT) — 1 day
- McCormick Place union crew coordination: StagePro to engage IATSE Local 2 for rigging, electrical, and carpentry

Either party may terminate this SOW without further liability (beyond amounts already paid) in the event of force majeure, which shall include but not be limited to: acts of God, government action, epidemic, pandemic, war, terrorism, labor disputes, **bankruptcy or insolvency of either party**, natural disaster, or other circumstances beyond the reasonable control of the affected party. Recovery of deposits already paid shall be governed by applicable bankruptcy law.
