**Risk Tier Definitions:**

- **Tier 2 (Revision):** Live asset with indirect reference to KnowGraph. Can remain live with revision to remove KnowGraph-specific language.
- **Tier 3 (Hold):** Draft or paused asset. No immediate action needed but must be reviewed before going live.
